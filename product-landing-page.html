About

Amelia has been interested in art ever since she was little and over the years has tried many different art forms.

She opened the Art Gallery to be able to connect people and have art from all over the world displayed.

The gallery has been opened since 2012 and has become very popular both in person and online. The gallery has been voted Top 20 in the Country, see the video below for more information on this

If you are looking to buy a piece, please head over to our Shop section. There is something for everybody. If you are looking for more information or you are an artist wishing to be showcased, please head over to the Contact Us section.
